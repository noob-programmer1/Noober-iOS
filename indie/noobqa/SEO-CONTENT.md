## Where to Host

**Option 1: `/blog` on your Vercel site** (recommended)
- Same domain = SEO juice stays on noobqa.com
- Add a `/blog` directory with static HTML posts
- Or use a simple SSG (Astro, Hugo) just for blog

**Option 2: Dev.to / Hashnode** (quick start)
- Post there first for immediate audience
- Cross-post to your blog with canonical URL
- Dev.to has built-in iOS/Swift audience

**Option 3: Medium** (not recommended)
- Paywalled content hurts SEO
- No control over canonical URLs

## Posting Schedule

- 1 blog post per week
- Cross-post to Dev.to + Hashnode
- Share on Twitter/LinkedIn (from build-in-public plan)
- First 5 posts should be the "high-intent" comparison pages
